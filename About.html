Our Story

At Coffee Haven, coffee isn't just a drink — it's an experience. Founded in 2015, we set out with a simple goal: serve the finest, ethically-sourced coffee in a cozy, welcoming space.

Our beans are carefully selected from farms around the world, roasted to perfection, and brewed with passion. Whether you're looking for a bold espresso or a smooth latte, our baristas craft each cup with love and skill.

Our Mission

To bring people together over great coffee, create a warm community space, and make a positive impact through sustainable practices.

Meet Our Team

Our team is a family of coffee enthusiasts, artists, and dreamers dedicated to making every visit special. Stop by and say hello!
